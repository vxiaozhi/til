# Research Plan: AI-Driven Vertical SaaS + API Products for AI Image & Video Generation

## Query Type: Depth-First + Breadth-First (Medium Complexity)

This is primarily a depth-first query analyzing one domain (AI image/video SaaS) from multiple angles (market, technical, competitive, business model), with breadth-first elements (image vs video subcategories require independent research).

## Research Objectives

Based on the existing report about indie dev profitable software, design 3-5 concrete AI-driven vertical SaaS + API products focused on AI image generation and AI video generation that can achieve stable profitability for a solo developer.

## Subagents (4 parallel)

### Agent 1: AI Image Generation - Market Landscape & Vertical Opportunities
- Research current AI image generation model landscape (Midjourney, Stable Diffusion, DALL-E, Flux, etc.)
- Identify underserved vertical niches for image AI (headshots, product photos, avatars, interior design, fashion, etc.)
- Find successful indie dev case studies in AI image space
- Analyze market size and growth trends for AI image verticals
- Use web search + wechat-article-search

### Agent 2: AI Video Generation - Market Landscape & Vertical Opportunities
- Research current AI video generation model landscape (Runway, Pika, Sora, Kling, Hailuo, etc.)
- Identify underserved vertical niches for video AI (ads, social media, training, presentations, etc.)
- Find successful indie dev case studies in AI video space
- Analyze market size and growth trends for AI video verticals
- Use web search + wechat-article-search

### Agent 3: Competitive Analysis - Existing Indie AI Image/Video Products
- Catalog existing indie/solo AI image and video SaaS products
- Analyze their features, pricing, target audience, and revenue (if public)
- Identify gaps and opportunities for new entrants
- Study why successful products work and failed ones don't
- Use web search + wechat-article-search

### Agent 4: Technical Architecture & Cost Analysis
- Research API costs for major AI image/video models
- Analyze infrastructure costs for running AI image/video SaaS
- Study optimal tech stack for solo AI SaaS development
- Calculate unit economics for different product types
- Use web search + wechat-article-search

## WeChat Article Search Strategy
- Search keywords: "AI生图", "AI视频", "AI SaaS", "独立开发者 AI", "AI image generation business", "AI video generation startup"
- Time range: past 12 months (2025-2026)
- Combine with web search for English-language sources

## Synthesis Approach
After all agents complete, synthesize findings to:
1. Identify top 5 vertical niches ranked by profit potential + feasibility for solo dev
2. Design 3-5 concrete product concepts with: target user, core feature, moat, pricing, revenue projection
3. Provide go-to-market strategy for each product
4. Recommend tech stack and cost estimates
