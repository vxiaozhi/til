# Research Plan: Additional Product Use Cases for Video Generation Pipeline

## Query Type: Breadth-First

Given the video generation pipeline is solved, identify additional vertical SaaS use cases where the same core technology can be repackaged for fast customer acquisition and profitability.

## Research Objectives

Identify 5-8 additional product use cases across different verticals, evaluate each on market size, competition, willingness to pay, technical fit, and speed to revenue. Prioritize by: fastest time-to-profit, highest revenue ceiling, and lowest competition.

## Subagents (4 parallel)

### Agent 1: Real Estate & Automotive Video Use Cases
- Real estate virtual tours, property listing videos, agent marketing
- Auto dealership vehicle walkaround videos
- Competition analysis, market size, pricing benchmarks

### Agent 2: Local Business & Service Industry Video Use Cases
- Restaurant menu video ads, food delivery
- Local services (plumbers, electricians, dentists, etc.)
- Gym/fitness, beauty/salon promo videos
- Competition, pricing, go-to-market

### Agent 3: Recruitment, Education & Corporate Video Use Cases
- Job listing videos, employer branding
- Course/training video creation
- Corporate internal communications
- Competition, pricing, market size

### Agent 4: Niche Consumer & Creator Video Use Cases
- UGC-style creator marketing videos
- Pet industry adoption/shelter videos
- Wedding/event highlight videos
- Toy/collectible animation
- Any other emerging niches
