# Research Plan: Free Image Prompt Website as Growth Engine for Paid Video SaaS

## Query Type: Depth-First

Research the feasibility of building a free AI image prompt website that serves as a traffic engine and lead generation tool for a paid e-commerce video ad generation SaaS.

## Core Hypothesis

A free, SEO-optimized AI image prompt directory/gallery/tool will attract high-volume organic traffic from people searching for AI image prompts. Strategic ad placement and contextual CTAs on this site will funnel a percentage of visitors to the paid video ad generation product. This creates a self-reinforcing growth loop: free content drives traffic → traffic sees paid product → conversions fund more free content.

## Subagents (4 parallel)

### Agent 1: Competitive Landscape & SEO Opportunity for Prompt Sites
- What free AI prompt sites already exist? (prompt galleries, prompt marketplaces, prompt generators)
- Analyze their SEO metrics: monthly traffic, top keywords, domain authority
- What keywords have high search volume + low competition?
- "AI image prompt" "Midjourney prompts" "Stable Diffusion prompts" "AI prompt generator" etc.
- What content formats rank best? (gallery, list, generator tool, blog)
- Which site structures attract backlinks naturally?

### Agent 2: "Free Tool → Paid Product" Growth Model Case Studies
- Successful examples: Revid.ai free mini-tools, HubSpot Website Grader, Canva free templates, Bannerbear free tools
- What conversion rates are typical? (free tool visitor → paid product trial → paid)
- How to design the bridge between the free tool and paid product?
- What ad placements and CTAs work best?
- Monetization alternatives for the prompt site itself (display ads, affiliate links to AI tools)

### Agent 3: Chinese Market Prompt Ecosystem
- WeChat article search for "AI提示词" "prompt网站" "AI绘画提示词"
- Chinese prompt sites and communities (Xiaohongshu prompt content, etc.)
- Cross-market traffic potential

### Agent 4: Technical Implementation & Growth Projections
- How to build the prompt site (static site? CMS? programmatic SEO?)
- Content strategy: how many prompts/pages needed for meaningful traffic?
- Content sourcing: manual curation vs AI-generated vs user-submitted
- Traffic projections: how long to reach 10K, 50K, 100K monthly visitors?
- Conversion funnel math: traffic → ad click → SaaS trial → paid
- Total build time for MVP
