# Research Plan: Optimal Product Form & GTM Strategy for E-commerce Video Ad Tool

## Query Type: Depth-First

Assuming the video generation pipeline is solved, what is the optimal product form factor, pricing strategy, and go-to-market approach to maximize user acquisition speed and profitability for a solo developer?

## Subagents (4 parallel)

### Agent 1: Product Form Factor Analysis
- Compare: Shopify App Store app vs standalone web app vs API-first vs Chrome extension
- For each form factor: user acquisition mechanics, revenue potential, development effort, platform dependency risk
- Best practices from successful Shopify apps
- Shopify App Store listing optimization, ranking factors, review dynamics
- Standalone web app SEO and distribution trade-offs

### Agent 2: Pricing, Packaging & Monetization Deep Dive
- Optimal pricing tiers, free trial vs freemium, credit systems analysis
- Per-video vs subscription vs hybrid models
- Competitor pricing comparison (all 15+ tools)
- Psychological pricing and conversion tactics
- How to price for maximum LTV while keeping CAC low
- Annual plan optimization, churn reduction tactics

### Agent 3: Landing Page, Conversion & Quick-Win GTM
- Landing page best practices for AI SaaS tools (with real examples)
- Conversion rate optimization specific to AI tools
- Quick-win distribution channels ranked by speed-to-revenue
- Reddit launch playbook, Product Hunt optimization
- SEO quick wins for video ad tool keywords
- Onboarding flow optimization

### Agent 4: Chinese Market GTM & WeChat Search
- WeChat article search for Chinese indie dev GTM strategies
- Chinese market pricing benchmarks for AI tools
- V2EX/知乎/小红书 distribution tactics
- 跨境卖家 community engagement strategies
