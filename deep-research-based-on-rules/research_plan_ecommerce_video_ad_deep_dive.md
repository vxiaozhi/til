# Research Plan: E-commerce Product Image to Video Ad Tool - Competitor & Market Deep Dive

## Query Type: Depth-First

Deep analysis of a single product category: tools that convert e-commerce product images into short video ads for social media (TikTok, Reels, Shorts).

## Research Objectives

Conduct a comprehensive competitor and market analysis for an AI-powered tool that transforms product photos into short video ads, targeting Shopify/WooCommerce/Amazon/eBay sellers.

## Subagents (4 parallel)

### Agent 1: Global Competitor Catalog & Feature Comparison
- Identify ALL existing tools that do product-to-video or e-commerce video ad generation
- Compare features, pricing, target audience, strengths/weaknesses
- Include both dedicated tools and adjacent competitors (Canva, CapCut, etc.)
- Find revenue data and user numbers where available

### Agent 2: Market Size, User Needs & Pain Points
- E-commerce video ad market size and growth trends
- What do e-commerce sellers actually need? What are their pain points?
- Video ad conversion data vs static images
- Platform-specific requirements (TikTok Shop, Instagram, YouTube)
- Willingness to pay data

### Agent 3: Chinese Market Competitors & Ecosystem
- Use wechat-article-search extensively
- Chinese e-commerce video ad tools market
- Douyin/TikTok Shop ecosystem requirements
- Cross-border e-commerce seller needs (Chinese sellers on Amazon/Shopify)

### Agent 4: Technical & UX Gap Analysis
- What features are ALL competitors missing?
- What makes the user experience bad in existing tools?
- Where can a solo developer win against funded competitors?
- API/multi-model integration opportunities
- Shopify App Store competitive landscape
